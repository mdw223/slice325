## Course Objectives

• **Educate clients with hypertension and diabetes on the importance of diet and nutrition** The primary objective of this course is to educate clients with hypertension and diabetes on the crucial role that diet, and nutrition play in managing their conditions.
By understanding the specific nutrient needs and dietary recommendations for each condition, clients can make informed choices about their food intake and develop healthy eating habits. This knowledge will empower clients to take control of their health and make lifestyle changes that contribute to better management of hypertension and diabetes.

• **Prevent falls by managing conditions effectively:** Another key objective of this course is to help clients with hypertension and diabetes prevent falls by managing their conditions effectively. By providing clients with information on the risk factors for falls and teaching them strategies for fall prevention, the course aims to reduce the likelihood of falls and their associated complications.
This includes addressing issues such as poor balance and muscle weakness, peripheral neuropathy, and low blood sugar through a combination of dietary modifications, exercise, and other lifestyle changes. By effectively managing their hypertension and diabetes, clients can minimize the risk of falls and improve their overall quality of life.
